We're Peregrine, the British clothing brand
keeping manufacturing miles to a

Since 1796, we've been crafting hard-working staples you can rely on, season after season. Be it
our sustainable menswear or womenswear - know where it comes from, see where it takes you.

Since 1796.

Our family-run business began life over 225 years
ago when our founder, Thomas Glover, began
knitting and selling jumpers in his hometown of
Leicestershire.


Eight generations later, one move to Manchester,
and a lot of lessons learned along the way, we're
still working with wool that's farmed just a few
miles from our factory.
